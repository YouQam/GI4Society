POPULATION MIGRATION

Project information:

This project "Population Migration" was done as a part of the course "Geoinformation in Society" in the winter semester 2018/2019 at ifgi, University of MÃ¼nster
by Dr.Auriol Degbelo and Dr.Tomi Kauppinen.

This project is a tool for comparing European countries in terms of the quality of life. This is, in terms of safety, housing conditions, economic conditions, level of happiness and the air pollution. The objective of the application is to support users in deciding which country is suitable for migration based on their preferences.
